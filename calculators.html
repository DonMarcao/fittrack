About 1RM:
One Rep Max is the maximum weight you can lift for a single repetition. This calculator uses the Epley formula to estimate your 1RM based on submaximal lifts.

Note: Use 1-10 reps for best accuracy. The closer to 1 rep, the more accurate the estimate.
